## 🎼 How to Create Your First Beat

1. **Open the Step Sequencer.** This is where you place drum sounds.  
2. **Select a drum sound** from the built-in list (kick, snare, hi-hat, etc.).  
3. **Click squares** in the sequencer grid to turn drum hits on or off.  
4. **Play the sequence** with the play button to hear your beat.  
5. **Add a melody** using the Piano Roll by clicking notes on a grid.  
6. Adjust volume and effects in the Mixer to balance your track.
